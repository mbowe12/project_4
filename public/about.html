About the music Any new music?

King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard have released 26 albums in 11 years.

That’s only counting albums they have released as KGLW, as members of the band are also involved in various other projects. That’s a LOT of output.

More often than not, the band also experiments with new sounds each album. So if you aren’t a fan of the most recent release there could be a new album around the corner that might be right up your alley.
